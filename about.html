Hi, my name is Maxwell Konnaris. I've always had an interest in human health, development, and performance which probably stems from my early days as a competitive athlete constantly dealing with injury. My education naturally followed this interest with a formal education in exercise physiology and research training in musculoskeletal injury, rehab, and regeneration. Over time, this curiosity led me to different areas of biological research such as cell and molecular biology and high throughput methods of genetic analysis. Eventually, I situated in my current role as a statistician and data scientist trained to develop statistical methods in the Silverman and Lazar Labs at Pennsylvania State University through the Bioinformatics PhD program. If you're interested in my journey or looking for advice on how to transition/merge your skills, follow my footsteps here.
